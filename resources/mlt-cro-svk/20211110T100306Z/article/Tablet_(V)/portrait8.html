FIFA WORLD CUP 2022 EUROPEAN QUALIFIERS   Group H

Malta showed great resilience as they twice came from behind to earn a point in their 2022 World Cup Group H qualifier against Cyprus on 11th October, 2021. A fine header by Zach Muscat (pictured above) early in the second half and Jurgen Degabriele's flick, deep into stoppage time, ensured a deserved point in a 2-2 draw for Devis Mangia's team to the delight of the vociferous Maltese fans, led by the South End Core, at the AEK Arena in Larnaca.
